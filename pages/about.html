About Our Website

This is the biggest university information based web media in Bangladesh.This was started its journey in the year 2020. The vision of the site to provide the information solution to the visitor students frequently for getting admission to their desired institute. But at the end of 2020 there grown a huge expectation from the visitors to serve them more & with new dimension. So this is reforms its service & came out with features & services. Now our motto is to establish as the complete admission solution around Bangladesh.

We have introduced unique services. The theme is same as like before but the criteria have been expanded vastly. Now we are dealing with almost all the University level educational institutes in our country & we are proudly announcing that we are only web portal in Bangladesh containing such huge information about educational institutes. As a part of our co-activities, we are participating with many ceremonies like convocation, admission fair, cultural programs etc. as web & media partner.

For our unique service we are now Google’s authorised level website.We have introduced for the first time the “Compare” service for private universities And “Public University admission ability search” based on GPA in our country.We are also providing information regarding how a person himself can apply abroad universities for higher education.
